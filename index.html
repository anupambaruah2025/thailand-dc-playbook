06 Power: The Binding Constraint

Demand didn't stall. Delivery did.

Over 90% of DC grid requests target the EEC, where 115kV transmission and substation capacity — not generation — is the bottleneck.

Generation
Thailand has surplus generation capacity — the constraint is downstream delivery, not supply.
EGAT Grid
EGAT has committed ~THB 3bn of immediate works and ~THB 30–31bn programmed against ~3,800MW of new demand.
EEC Substation (congested)
115/230kV lines and substation bays are exhausted. Utility substations take 24–36 months to deliver. Over 90% of new grid requests target this node.
Second-Ring Substation (healthy)
Substation headroom exists — the Energy Ministry is openly steering projects here.
